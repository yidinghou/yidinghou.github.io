Yiding Hou | Mon 10 September 2018

Tracking Daily Fantasy Sports¶

Daily Fantasy Sports (DFS) is a fantasy sports format where you can win money by creating a lineup of the highest scoring fantasy sports players and entering daily fantasy contests. The top placing lineups in a contest can pay out huge sums of money. I started tracking the results of DFS players for the MLB 2018 season and here are some of my findings.

About 12% of MLB DFS players are winners. Of the winning players, the top 1% account for 33% percent of all profits and 66% of all the contest entries. Below is a graph of the profits of four of the most successful players historically.

Below is a graph of the ROI for these four players. The purple filled line is the returns from the market in the same period following the investment patterns of one of the pros ("mazwa"). A couple pros ("mazwa" and "shawnzhan") are having succesful MLB seasons and beating the market returns. A long shot dream of mine is to play DFS fulltime as a job. However, after tracking the results and looking at the top pros, it looks like this may be infeasilbe. Only a few of the top players are winning lots of money, and even fewer are beating the stock market.
